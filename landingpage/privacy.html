Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 19, 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how NetWatcher ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, stores, and protects your personal information when you use our hosted services at netwatcher.io (the landing site) and app.netwatcher.io (the application platform).

This policy applies only to the hosted versions of NetWatcher operated by us. If you self-host NetWatcher using our open-source software, your data handling practices are entirely under your own control, and this policy does not apply to your self-hosted instance.

2. Data Controller

The data controller for personal data collected through our hosted services is:

NetWatcher

Email: hello@netwatcher.io

3. Data We Collect

3.1 Account Information

When you register on app.netwatcher.io, we collect:

  • Email address
  • Name (if provided)
  • Password (stored securely using industry-standard hashing)

3.2 Login & Session Data

We track login events for security purposes and to improve the service. This includes:

  • Timestamp of login
  • IP address at login
  • Browser user agent string
  • Session duration

This data helps us detect unauthorized access, prevent abuse, and improve site reliability.

3.3 Analytics Data (Rybbit)

We use Rybbit, a privacy-focused, self-hosted analytics platform, to collect anonymous usage data on both netwatcher.io and app.netwatcher.io. Rybbit collects:

  • Page views and navigation paths
  • Referrer information
  • Browser type and screen size
  • Country-level geolocation (derived from IP, IP is not stored)

Rybbit is self-hosted on our own infrastructure and does not share data with any third parties. No personal information is collected through Rybbit, and no cross-site tracking is performed.

3.4 Network Monitoring Data

When using app.netwatcher.io, the agents you deploy collect network performance data (latency, packet loss, traceroutes, etc.) about the targets you configure. This data belongs to you and is stored in your workspace.

3.5 Usage Data

We may collect general usage information such as:

  • Features used and frequency
  • Workspace and agent configurations (metadata only)
  • Error reports and performance metrics

4. How We Use Your Data

We use the collected data for the following purposes only:

  • Providing the service — Authenticating you, managing your account, delivering monitoring data
  • Security — Detecting unauthorized access, preventing abuse, protecting against fraud
  • Service improvement — Understanding usage patterns, identifying bugs, improving the user experience
  • Analytics — Aggregated, anonymous insights about site usage via Rybbit for our own internal purposes
  • Communication — Sending service-related emails (account verification, password resets, critical alerts)

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with advertisers or third-party data brokers.

5. Analytics — Rybbit

We believe in transparent, privacy-respecting analytics. Our analytics platform, Rybbit, is:

  • Self-hosted — All analytics data stays on our infrastructure
  • Privacy-focused — No personal identifiers, no fingerprinting, no cross-site tracking
  • Cookie-free — Rybbit does not use cookies for tracking
  • Lightweight — Minimal impact on page performance

We use Rybbit solely for our own analytical purposes — to understand how visitors interact with our site and application so we can make improvements.

6. Cookies

Our hosted services use a minimal number of cookies:

  • Session cookies — Required for authentication and keeping you logged in on app.netwatcher.io. These are essential for the service to function.
  • Security cookies — Used for CSRF protection and session integrity.

We do not use advertising cookies, tracking cookies, or any third-party cookies. Rybbit operates without cookies.

7. Data Retention

  • Account data — Retained for as long as your account is active. Deleted upon account deletion request.
  • Login/session logs — Retained for up to 12 months for security audit purposes, then automatically purged.
  • Analytics data — Aggregated analytics data is retained indefinitely; no personal data is associated with it.
  • Network monitoring data — Retained according to the data retention settings in your workspace. You may delete your data at any time.

8. Your Rights (GDPR & Applicable Law)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), the United Kingdom, or any other jurisdiction with applicable data protection laws, you have the following rights:

  • Right of Access — Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Right to Rectification — Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Right to Erasure — Request deletion of your personal data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to Data Portability — Request your data in a structured, machine-readable format
  • Right to Object — Object to the processing of your personal data for certain purposes
  • Right to Restrict Processing — Request that we limit how we use your data
  • Right to Withdraw Consent — Where processing is based on consent, withdraw it at any time
  • Right to Lodge a Complaint — File a complaint with your local data protection authority (DPA)

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at hello@netwatcher.io. We will respond within 30 days.

9. Third-Party Services

We use a minimal number of third-party services:

  • Rybbit (self-hosted) — Privacy-focused analytics. No data leaves our infrastructure.
  • SMTP provider — For sending transactional emails (account verification, password resets). Only your email address is shared with the mail provider for delivery purposes.

We do not integrate with advertising platforms, social media trackers, or data brokers.

10. Self-Hosted Instances

NetWatcher is open-source software (AGPL-3.0). If you choose to self-host NetWatcher on your own infrastructure:

  • This Privacy Policy does not apply to your self-hosted instance
  • You are the data controller for all data processed by your instance
  • You are responsible for your own privacy policy and data protection compliance
  • We have no access to data on your self-hosted instance

11. Data Security

We take the security of your data seriously and implement appropriate technical and organizational measures, including:

  • Encryption in transit (TLS/HTTPS) for all connections
  • Secure password hashing using industry-standard algorithms
  • Access controls and authentication for internal systems
  • Regular security reviews

While we strive to protect your data, no method of internet transmission or electronic storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

12. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed at individuals under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If we become aware that we have collected personal data from a child under 16, we will take steps to delete it promptly.

13. International Data Transfers

Our servers are located in Canada. If you access our services from outside Canada, your data may be transferred to, stored, and processed in Canada. By using our services, you consent to the transfer of your data to Canada, which may have different data protection laws than your country of residence.

14. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. If we make material changes, we will provide prominent notice on the site or via email.

We encourage you to review this page periodically for the latest information on our privacy practices.

15. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, want to exercise your data rights, or have concerns about our data practices, please contact us:

Email: hello@netwatcher.io
